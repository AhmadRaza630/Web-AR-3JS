How to use AR menu

Simple flow: home page se food choose karein, phir AR screen se model ko surface par place karein.

Steps:
  1. Is page ko Android Chrome ya iPhone Safari me open karein.
  2. Apna favourite food item select karein.
  3. AR screen open ho gi, wahan se “View in AR” dabayein.
  4. Camera se flat surface scan karein & model place kar dein.
HTTPS hosting + AR supported device required for best experience.
